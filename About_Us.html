About Us

Introducing Philfauna



The primary goal of Philfauna is to safeguard and preserve the wonders of the Philippines' natural environment. It tries to teach the community on how to be environmentally attentive and to have the right understanding about the endemic animals in the Philippines. It aims to captive the minds and hearts of the community on how wonderful the nature that can be found in the Philippines.It also functions as an online platform, for those who lacks the ability to see the animals up close without the dangers of the animals being in captivity inside the cages.
